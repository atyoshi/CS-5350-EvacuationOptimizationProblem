    STEP 1: CHECK WHO JUST ARRIVED
       For every group currently moving:
           If their arrival time has come:
               - Take them off the "on the move" list
               - Free up space on the path they used
               - If they reached a SAFE ZONE:
                     Add them to "Evacuated" count
               - Otherwise:
                     Add them to the room they just entered

       If everyone is now safe - Done! All evacuees are safe!

    STEP 2: SEND PEOPLE OUT FROM ROOMS
       Did anyone move this round? - Not yet

       For each room in our top-down order:
           If there are people waiting in this room:

               - Look at the first step: Room A -> Hallway B
               - Find how many people can fit at the moment
